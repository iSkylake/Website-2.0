"There's always room for improvement" Hi my name is George Yu and I'm a Software Engineer

About

Hey! I'm a computer enthusiast and programmer who is always looking to learn more and try new tehcnologies. I got my first interest with computers when I was 4 years old just clicking icons and writing words in the Notepad. Later when I tried my first computer video game and used the Internet for the first time with Netscape, I discovered a new whole world.

Back in Highschool we didn't have any programming class, the closest thing was designing a website using HTML and CSS. I remember adding a lot of cool images and GIFs and messing with the style, at the end it looked like some kind of tumblr. In College is where I touched programming for the first time learning different languages like C, C++, Java, VB, C#, etc. and solving complex engineering problems with data structures. Another fascinating thing was playing with Arduino, programming a microcontroller to do things in the real world.

After I finished College, I came to San Francisco at the beginning of 2016.
